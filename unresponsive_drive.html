Unresponsive Drive

Sometimes disks become unresponsive due to a bad replacement procedure or the storage array cannot detect it. Unresponsive drives are automatically power-cycled to see whether the fault condition can be cleared. If the condition cannot be cleared, the drive is flagged as failed.

  • Check MEL for any event against the related drive.
  • If nothing is found, then check for any FW that deals with unresponsive status.
  • Reseat
  • Replace Drive

Note :This drive cannot be manually failed since the controllers are not able to communicate with the drive. All what need to do is to remove the drive and replace it.

Note :Wait at least two minutes between removing the prolematic drive and inserting the replacement drive in order to allow the array to detect the change.
