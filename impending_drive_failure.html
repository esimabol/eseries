Impending Drive failure

  • You may need to manually fail drive before replacing.
  • To manually fail a drive with hot spare available, make sure the copy content box is selected. Once failed the hot spare disk will change highlighted color to represent it is in use.
  • Check operations in progress to see process details. If no hot spare is avaialble, you may uncheck this box.
  • If drive reconstruction will not start automatically after replacement, you may use the Manually reconstruct option. The Replace option may be needed if Manual reconstruction is not available.
