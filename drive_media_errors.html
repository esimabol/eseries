Drive Reports Media Errors

Unreadable sector means is that there are sectors on the drive(s) in the RAID 5 that have both the data and parity unreadable. Therefore, its logging unreadable sectors and data loss. the Physical Unreadable sector means that the sector is physically bad on the drive. The logical means that was reconstructed but the data or parity was not available to complete the stripe.

  • Check Drive status in Storage array profile
  • Check MEL for media errors
  • If you find more than two or three unrecoverable media errors on a drive in that state you can replace it.
